jBASIC Language Overview jBASIC, sometimes referred to as jBC is a BASIC style language modeled after PICK system. It is used mostly to build business applications that can utilize a multivalue database or a third party dbms depending on the configuration it is given.  Benefits of jBASIC  jBASIC comes with a built in debugger.
